QUESTION 3

Provide an overview of freshwater and saltwater fish with two examples for each.

Enter your answers in the textbox below then check by clicking 'REVEAL'

Freshwater fish

Overview: The term ‘freshwater fish’ incorporates all varieties that live in fresh water. Most freshwater fish are oily fish.
  • Trout
  • Carp

Saltwater fish

Overview: The term ‘saltwater fish’ includes any variety that lives in the ocean and estuaries. Many of these fish are white-fleshed fish and have a clean, fresh taste; however, many cheaper species are oily fish and have a softer texture and slightly stronger taste
  • Sole
  • Snapper
  • Herring
  • Mullet
