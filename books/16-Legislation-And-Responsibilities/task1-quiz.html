  • To avoid civil action – both personal and against the business.
  • To ensure the safe delivery, storage, preparation, cooking and service of food, in order to demonstrate due diligence.

QUESTION 2

Legislation requires all food businesses to implement a Food Safety Management Plan encompassing HACCP. Before this plan can be written, other factors need to be considered. Describe four of these factors.

Enter your answers in the text box below then check by clicking 'REVEAL'

Add another answer

Now check your answers above to the correct answers below.

  • Buildings and equipment are fit for use.
  • Pest control policies are written.
  • Staff are trained.
  • Suppliers are vetted for their own food safety measures.
  • Stock control measures are in place.
  • An effective cleaning schedule has been developed.
Note: In all the above cases, the procedures must be recorded by the food business. This will allow the business to prove, if necessary, compliance with legislation.
