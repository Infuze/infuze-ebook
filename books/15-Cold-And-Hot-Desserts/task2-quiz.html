Explain the following techniques.

Enter your answers in the text box below then check by clicking 'REVEAL'

Emulsifying

‘Emulsifying’ describes the process of combining two ingredients that don’t naturally mix. This technique is used when making a cake batter – after creaming the sugar and butter together, the eggs are added through the technique of emulsification.

Blending

Blending is a technique used to combine two or more ingredients together to create a uniform mixture. A good example of this is when making a pancake batter for a crêpe Suzette.
