QUESTION 2

Why does puff pastry ‘puff up’?

Enter your answers in the text box below then check by clicking 'REVEAL'

Add another answer

Now check your answers above to the correct answers below.

This type of light and flaky textured pastry puffs up when cooked due to the alternating layers of fat and dough created during production. While cooking, the water in the dough turns into steam and pushes the layers of fat while trying to escape. This process will raise the pastry which will stay up when fully cooked.
