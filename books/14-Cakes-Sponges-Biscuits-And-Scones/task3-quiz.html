Sugar batter

  • The fat and sugar are mixed to produce a light and aerated cream
  • Eggs are beaten and added to the mix slowly
  • The remaining ingredients are then folded into the mix  folding prevents the aeration from collapsing

Melting method

  • Eggs and sugar are whisked into a soft ribbon
  • The other ingredients are folded into the mix
  • The final stage is for the butter, margarine or oil to be poured into the mix
