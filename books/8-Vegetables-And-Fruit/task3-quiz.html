QUESTION 1

What is the purpose of ‘par cooking’ certain vegetables?

Now check your answers above to the correct answers below.

Par cooking is a process that is often used to allow vegetables to retain optimal colour. They are blanched in boiling water for a short period of time, refreshed in cold water and then drained and stored. They can then be quickly reheated in a pan with oil and/or butter and seasonings for service.
