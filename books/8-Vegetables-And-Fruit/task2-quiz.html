Commodities Processes Explanation
Example: Garlic Peeled and sliced or chopped. Often stored in oil to prevent discolouration through oxygen; used as a basic flavouring.
Onions
Onions are diced and sliced for a multitude of dishes and are often pre-fried in butter without colour to speed up the cooking process during service and improve storage.
Herbs
Fresh herbs are sliced or chopped and used as a flavouring agent and as garnish.
Flavourings
Flavourings are used to enhance the taste of vegetables and fruit. Common types are herbs, soy sauce, curry pastes and stock syrup, cinnamon or vanilla for fruit.
Stuffing
Stuffing vegetables such as courgettes or tomatoes means they need to be de-seeded or hollowed out first. Flat mushrooms can also be stuffed and then grilled. Capsicum can be stuffed with rice and then braised
