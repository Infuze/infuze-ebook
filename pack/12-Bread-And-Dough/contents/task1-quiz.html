QUESTION 4

Glazing a dough using different commodities will result in a different finish. Describe the finishes of each of the following glazes.

Enter your answers in the textbox below then check by clicking 'REVEAL'

Milk and salt water

This will darken the final product’s crust.

Butter or other oils

This will change the flavour of the bread slightly and also provide a golden crust. A good example of this is an olive bread.

Egg glaze

Using egg glazes will provide a glossy finish to the product. This glaze is also used when binding other commodities to the bread such as oats and seeds, as in a challah bread.
